Cambridge has [open data](https://bluebikes.com/system-data) on bycicle rentals, so I thought I'd visualize it to get
the pulse of the city through bike activity. I was also curious to find out which bike stations
are busier (spoiler alert: the ones close to Harvard/MIT) and what are the bike rush hours.
